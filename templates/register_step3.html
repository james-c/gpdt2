Control Over Your Data

You have the power to decide how your data is used. Whether it's contributing to specific types of research or opting out entirely, your choices are respected and implemented by the GPDT2 Trust.

The Impact of Your Decisions

By allowing your data to be used for research, you're contributing to advancements in medical science. Your data could help in developing new treatments, understanding diseases better, and improving healthcare outcomes. However, we ensure that your privacy is never compromised.

Setting Your Preferences

In the following steps, you'll have the opportunity to set your data usage preferences. We'll guide you through each category of research and explain what your data could be used for, giving you the information needed to make informed decisions.
