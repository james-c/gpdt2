Your participation is voluntary, and you retain full control over the use of your data. At any point, you can withdraw your consent or adjust your preferences through your member dashboard.

This process not only facilitates the efficient and secure use of data for health research but also ensures that any data usage aligns with your personal choices and legal rights.
