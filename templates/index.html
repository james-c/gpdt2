Welcome to the GPDT2 Portal

This portal serves as a demonstration of the General Practice Data Trust part 2 (GPDT2), aiming to showcase the features and capabilities we're building to empower patients and improve healthcare outcomes through ethical and secure data use.

As it stands, the GPDT2 demo is a dynamic representation of how data governance and patient consent can intersect within a digital framework. Our goal is to illustrate a future where patients have full transparency and control over how their medical data is used for research, without compromising their privacy or autonomy.

Key features currently implemented in this demo include:

  • Consent Management: Interactive tools allowing users to specify and modify their consent preferences for different types of research.
  • Data Audit Logs: A comprehensive view of when and how patient data has been accessed, adhering to consent guidelines.
  • Governance Overview: Insight into the governance structure of GPDT2, including the roles and responsibilities of board members.
