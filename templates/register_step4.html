Who governs the use of data within the GPDT2 Trust?

Question: Is it data scientists and researchers?

Answer: No, the use of data within the GPDT2 Trust is governed by an independent board comprising patients, healthcare professionals, and data privacy experts.

Can you change your data sharing preferences after joining the trust?

Question: Are preferences final once set?

Answer: No, you can change your data sharing preferences at any time through your member dashboard.

What types of research can your data be used for?

Question: Can it be used for marketing and commercial purposes?

Answer: No, your data can only be used for research projects you explicitly agree to. It cannot be used for marketing and commercial purposes.

How does the GPDT2 Trust protect your data privacy?

Question: Is it by sharing data with all interested parties without restrictions?

Answer: No, the GPDT2 Trust protects your data privacy by anonymizing data before it's used in research. Your personal information is never shared without your explicit consent.

In case of a data breach, what measures are in place?

Question: Are data breach incidents only reported annually?

Answer: No, in case of a data breach, immediate notification is sent to affected members and steps are taken to secure data and prevent further breaches.

Who can access your personal data within the GPDT2 Trust?

Question: Can any internet user access my data?

Answer: No, only authorized researchers for approved projects can access your personal data. Your data is never shared publicly or with unauthorized parties.

What is your role in the governance of the GPDT2 Trust?

Question: Do members have no role in governance?

Answer: No, members can vote on key decisions and stand for governance positions. Your voice and participation are crucial in guiding the trust's direction.
