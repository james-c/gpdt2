Our Mission

The GPDT2 Trust aims to facilitate medical research that can lead to breakthroughs in treatment and patient care. At the same time, we stand firm in our commitment to protecting the privacy and rights of our patients. Your data will only be used in ways you're comfortable with, under strict ethical standards.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data can help researchers understand diseases better, develop new treatments, and improve patient care. However, you have full control over which types of research your data can be used for. We'll guide you through setting your preferences in the next steps.

Our Governance Structure

The Trust is overseen by a governance board made up of patients, healthcare professionals, and data privacy experts. This diverse group ensures that all decisions made about data use are balanced, ethical, and in the best interest of patients and research.
