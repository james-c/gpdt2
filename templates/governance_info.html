Governance Structure

  • Overview of Governance Structure

    The GPDT2 trust is governed by a board consisting of elected patient representatives, healthcare professionals, and data privacy experts. This structure ensures balanced decision-making with the interests of all stakeholders in mind.

Recent Decisions

  • 15 November 2023 - Adoption of New Data Privacy Policy

    On 15 November 2023, the board voted to adopt a new data privacy policy that increases protections for patient data, restricting access to only vetted research projects. This decision reflects our ongoing commitment to data security and patient privacy.

  • 10 December 2023 - Expansion of Research Categories

    On 10 December 2023, the board approved the expansion of research categories eligible for data use. This expansion includes rare disease research and environmental health studies, broadening the scope of impactful research supported by our trust.

  • 20 January 2024 - Implementation of Quarterly Transparency Reports

    On 20 January 2024, the board decided to begin publishing quarterly transparency reports. These reports aim to detail the trust's activities, such as data use approvals, project outcomes, and governance changes, ensuring transparency and trust member engagement.

  • 05 February 2024 - Introduction of Patient Advisory Panels

    On 05 February 2024, to further involve patients in the governance process, the board approved the creation of Patient Advisory Panels. These panels serve as a platform for direct patient input on proposed projects and policy changes, emphasizing the importance of patient voices in our decision-making process.

  • 28 February 2024 - Enhanced Data Security Measures

    On 28 February 2024, acknowledging the critical importance of safeguarding patient data, the board ratified enhanced data security measures. These measures include the adoption of state-of-the-art encryption technologies and stringent access controls, aimed at preventing unauthorized access and data breaches.
